Flappy Bird!

About the Game

In 2014, the indie game Flappy Bird surged to the top of the Apple App Store and Google Play Store charts. In this deceptively simple side-scrolling mobile game, you propel a tiny flapping bird through a gauntlet of green pipes above and below you. If you happen to collide with one of these pipes just once, the game is instantly over—no lives, no health, no second chances.

The game looks basic at first, but you will find that upon initial play you will struggle to even fetch a double-digit score as you watch your winged avatar fall to the ground time and time again. The simple premise and retro 8-bit graphics conceal the difficulty of this surprise smash hit.

Do you have what it takes to fall over and over again, and get back up and keep trying? If so, try your hand at Flappy Bird and see if you can beat your friend's high score!
